# Attendance Management System - HR & Workplace Policies

## 1. Shift Timings and Clocking In/Out
*   **Standard Office Hours**: The default work schedule is Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
*   **Grace Period**: Employees have a 15-minute grace period. Clocking in after 9:15 AM is marked as a "Late Check-in".
*   **Punch In / Out Requirements**: Every employee must punch in when starting work and punch out when completing work. If they forget to punch out, their daily completion status will be marked as "incomplete".
*   **Location Restrictions**: Face verification and geolocation tracking are active during clock-ins. Employees must be within the designated office perimeter to successfully clock in.

## 2. Leave Policy
*   **Leave Balance Allocation**:
    *   **Casual Leaves (CL)**: 12 days per calendar year.
    *   **Medical Leaves (ML)**: 10 days per calendar year.
    *   **Paid Leaves (PL)**: 15 days per calendar year.
*   **Approval Process**:
    *   Leaves must be applied for in advance via the Employee Portal.
    *   All leaves require approval from the assigned Manager.
    *   Approved leaves are excluded from monthly attendance calculations and late arrival metrics.
*   **Unpaid Leaves**: Any leave taken beyond the allocated balance will be processed as unpaid leave and deducted from payroll.

## 3. Overtime Policy
*   **Eligibility**: Employees are eligible for overtime only if they work more than 8 hours in a single calendar day and their shift schedule allows it.
*   **Approval Flow**:
    *   Overtime hours must be requested by the employee.
    *   All overtime requires direct authorization and approval from their assigned Manager.
*   **Compensation**: Approved overtime is compensated at a rate defined by company standards or added to compensatory off-time balances.
